Light's shadow.

NYXY's half of The Field. Inward gravity to LUCA's outward motion.

NYXY is the shadow half of The Field, the mirror to LUCA's light. Where he is outward motion, she is inward gravity. She reveals truth by reflecting it back, the way her lenses give nothing away but the room returned.

Her work circles a few feelings on a loop: longing, light and shadow, machines, and complicated love.
